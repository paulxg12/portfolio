Quantum Computing

Quantum Lab Buddy

Visual quantum gate simulator with Bloch sphere visualization for 1-n qubits, enabling interactive exploration of quantum computing concepts.

Overview

An interactive quantum computing simulator designed to make quantum mechanics accessible and visual. The platform allows users to build and simulate quantum circuits with support for 1 to n qubits, providing real-time visualization of quantum states.

Built as an educational tool, it helps students and researchers understand quantum gate operations through intuitive visual feedback and mathematical representations.

Key Features

  • Multi-Qubit Support: Simulate circuits from 1 to n qubits
  • Bloch Sphere Visualization: 3D representation of qubit states
  • Gate Operations: Support for H, X, Y, Z, S, T, CNOT, and custom gates
  • Probability Charts: Real-time measurement outcome probabilities
  • Circuit Diagrams: Visual circuit representation
  • Gate Matrix Preview: Display matrix representation of gates
  • Bell State Selection: Generate entangled states easily
  • Simulation Animator: Step-through circuit execution
